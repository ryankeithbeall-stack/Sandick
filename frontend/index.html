Launch your own vault

Deploy a fresh ERC-4626 basket vault through the on-chain VaultFactory. You become the operator; the platform fee is wired in automatically.

  1. 1

    Pick your basket

    Select the HIP-3 coins and equal-weight them — or bring custom weights. The flagship SANDICK is just one configuration.

  2. 2

    Call createVault

    The factory deploys your BasketVault, registers it, and stamps the platform fee + treasury. You’re set as owner (operator).

  3. 3

    Set fees & go live

    Configure your own mgmt / performance / exit fees, allow-list assets, and open deposits. The platform’s cut is governed separately and can’t be removed.
